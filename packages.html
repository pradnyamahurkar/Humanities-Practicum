Packages

Want to plan a trip? We offer packages, within your budget, customized to meet your requirements. You don't have to worry about booking you flight tickets, car rentals, and hotels separately. We give you all-in-one package options and you choose the one that you like the most! We will also put together a travel itinerary for you so that you can enjoy your trip to the fullest.

The BeaconChange Way

We like to offer our clients customized packages. The process of creating a package is as follows:

  • Understand the client's requirements.
  • Provide our clients a list of destinations they might like.
  • Get the best possible discounts for our clients.
  • Integrate a little surprise gift in the package for special occasions.
  • Provide the client sight-seeing options.

Some Pre-Made Packages

Although we believe in creating customized packages for our clients, we have some pre-made packages too:

Mauritius

Mauritius is the perfect vacation destination for beach lovers. You can enjoy water sports such as scuba diving, swimming, and parasailing in the clear blue water! Alternatively, you can relax and watch the sunset. The package includes:

  • Duration: 6 nights and 7 days stay
  • Island tours (Duration: 8 hours)
  • Accomodation at a luxury resort
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Spa
  • Airport transfers

Thailand

Enjoy the Thai culture and food. From mountains to beaches, you can experience different type of terrains. You can visit the beautiful temples and practice meditation too. Fun fact: you can have bubble tea everyday in Thailand! The package includes:

  • Duration: 4 nights and 5 days
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Bangkok City tour
  • Coral Beach tour
  • Economy flight tickets from Mumbai, India
  • Airport transfers

Goa, India

Goa is one of the prime tourist attractions in India. It is famous for its beaches, parties, and casinos. You can get a variety of Indian sea food and also experience the rich fisherman culture in Goa. The package includes:

  • Duration: 3 nights and 4 days
  • Accomodation at a resort
  • Breakfast, lunch, and dinner
  • 1 hour boat cruise with live music
  • Sight-seeing
  • Airport transfers

Kerala, India

Feel the fresh tropican breez in Kerala! Explore the beaches, hillstations, waterfalls, and lagoons during your trip. You can find a variety of animals like elephants, tigers, and crocodiles when you visit the wildlife sanctuaries. Tha package includes:

  • Duration: 7 nights and 8 days
  • Free WiFi
  • Accomodation in a 5-star hotel
  • Sight-seeing
  • A selection of Indian, Chinese, and continental delights
